Simple. We connect to your Basecamp account, grab all the To-Do items in all the To-Do lists, across all your projects and show them to you in one sortable easy to read list. See a Screenshot

Do you have a time machine? Neither do we. We were sick of losing time searching for To-Dos and sorting them in our heads. With BlueRidge, you can see everything at once. The way it should be.
