What does BlueRidge do?

Simple. We connect to your Basecamp account, grab all the To-do items in all the To-do lists, across all your projects and show them to you in one sortable easy to read list.

Why would I want it?

Do you have a time machine? Neither do we. We were sick of losing time searching for To-dos and sorting them in our heads. With BlueRidge, you can see everything at once. The way it should be.

How much does it cost?

A fraction of what it’s worth. You’ll save that time in the first day using it. No hidden fees. No minimum terms. Cancel anytime. See pricing details.

What else does it do?

Nothing. This isn’t another To-do list app or a replacement for Basecamp. But it does make using Basecamp To-dos a whole helluva lot easier.
