What’s all this, anyway?

We had a problem.

We realized we were losing time and money because we couldn’t get a good overview of all our To-dos at once. So we created this. It does a pretty good job of solving our problem. We beleive it will help you, too.

Super-alpha mode.

Things are still a little rough around here. We just moved in and are still unpacking. Don’t expect too much right away. If you see a bug, let us know.

Great things are coming.

The list of things to add is quite long. Here are a few of them:

  • An overview of how many To-dos are assigned to each team member.
  • Ability to assign due dates and people to To-dos directly in BlueRidge.
  • Integration with other project management systems.
  • Sorting of To-dos by person, project, list or date.
  • “Mute” people to temporarily hide them from the current view. And…
  • “Solo” people to temporarily show only their To-dos.

Your suggestions are welcome. Just add them to our list.
